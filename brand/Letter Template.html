Statement of Work — Cloud Cost & Commitment Review (Northwind · Jun 2026)

Dear Helena,

Thank you for the time on Wednesday. Following the conversation between yourself, Marcus, and the platform team, this letter sets out the proposed engagement to take a clear, commercially-honest look at Northwind's AWS and Azure run-rate — and a credible plan to bring it back into line.

In short: we believe there is between 22% and 30% of measurable annual saving available to Northwind across the two clouds, recoverable inside a single quarter, without de-scoping the programme that's currently in flight.

// scope of work

  • Spend & commitment baseline across AWS (eu-west-2, us-east-1) and Azure (uksouth, westeurope) for the trailing 90 days.
  • Top-20 recommendation backlog — right-size, lifecycle, modernise, retire — prioritised by net annual saving and effort.
  • Reserved Instances, Savings Plans and Azure Hybrid Benefit plan, sized to ≤85% of rolling baseline.
  • Executive read-out plus a working session with the platform & data teams to land the first wave.

The work runs in three phases over six elapsed weeks. We embed inside Northwind's existing standups — no parallel governance, no shadow programme. Your team owns the changes; we own the analysis and the commercial shape.
