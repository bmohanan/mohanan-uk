// 04 — Voice

brand/voice

Sound like

+Plain English. Short sentences. Verbs that do something.
+Commercially fluent — talk to CFOs and SREs in the same paragraph.
+Specific numbers. Real outcomes. Quiet confidence.

Don't sound like

−Vendor copy. "Synergistic", "leverage", "best-in-class".
−Hyperscaler marketing. Don't quote their slide decks.
−Stack rankings of every certification you have.

House phrases

"Make hyperscalers pay back their promise.
"Strategy, delivery, and the commercial fluency to do both.
"Day-two leadership most cloud programmes forget to budget for.
